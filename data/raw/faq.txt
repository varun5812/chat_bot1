Customer Support Knowledge Base

Password Reset
Customers can reset their password from the login page by selecting "Forgot password". A secure reset link will be sent to the registered email address. The link expires after 30 minutes.

Refund Policy
Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase if the product or subscription is eligible. The customer should contact support with the order ID and reason for the refund request.

Shipping Policy
Standard shipping usually takes 5 to 7 business days. Express shipping usually takes 2 to 3 business days. Shipping times may vary by region and public holidays.

Billing Support
For billing questions, customers should provide their account email, invoice number, and payment date. Support can help with duplicate charges, failed payments, and invoice copies.

Support Hours
Customer support is available Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM. Requests submitted outside business hours will be reviewed on the next business day.

Account Deactivation
Customers can request account deactivation by contacting support from their registered email address. The support team verifies ownership before processing the request.

Escalation
If a customer reports fraud, account takeover, data loss, or legal concerns, the issue should be escalated to a human support specialist.
